About Delhi Dehradun Expressway

The Delhi–Dehradun Expressway is a highway that connects Delhi with Dehradun. It is being developed to make travel faster and more convenient for people travelling between Delhi, Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand.

Currently, the journey between Delhi and Dehradun takes several hours due to traffic and road conditions. This expressway aims to reduce travel time and improve road safety for commuters.

The route passes through important areas like Baghpat and Saharanpur, and is expected to improve connectivity and make travel smoother for both tourists and local travellers.


Important Details

• Length: Around 210 km
• Route: Delhi - Uttar Pradesh - Uttarakhand
• Purpose: Faster transportation and better connectivity
